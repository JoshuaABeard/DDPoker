Practice Mode - Learn and Improve

Master Texas Hold'em tournament play against realistic AI opponents. Practice in a risk-free environment with full control over game settings and learning tools.

Realistic AI Opponents

The AI players simulate realistic human behavior patterns, including position awareness, pot odds calculation, and even psychological factors like tilt and boredom. This helps you prepare for real tournament play against actual opponents, not perfect poker robots.

  • Configurable AI personalities (tight/loose, aggressive/passive)
  • Customizable hand selection criteria
  • Position and pot odds awareness
  • Stack size considerations
  • Draw-chasing tendencies
  • Tilt and boredom simulation

DD Dashboard

Customizable sidebar displays real-time information to help you make better decisions:

  • Poker Clock (blinds, antes, level, time remaining)
  • Upcoming blinds and antes
  • Your current best hand
  • Pot odds and hand strength
  • Odds to improve your hand
  • Tournament rank and table count
  • Calculator tool access
  • AI Advisor recommendations

AI Advisor

Get context-aware recommendations with clear explanations. The Advisor considers table position, stack size, and hand strength - factors you can actually use at a real table.

Tournament Formats

Practice with 15 pre-defined tournament structures or create your own custom formats. Support for limit, pot limit, and no limit Hold'em.

  • Configurable buy-ins, rebuys, and add-ons
  • Custom blind and ante schedules
  • Variable level duration
  • Prize structure configuration
  • Export tournament data to spreadsheets

Interface Features

  • Full screen or resizable window mode
  • Adjustable dashboard, chat, and table layout
  • 4-color deck with customizable backs and table colors
  • Keyboard shortcuts for all actions
  • Chat area with action history
  • Streamlined betting interface (no pop-ups)

Learning Options

Training tools to help you learn and experiment:

  • Show Winning Hand - See the hand that won each pot
  • Show Folded Hands - Find out if your bluff worked
  • Show Mucked Hands - See losing hands that went to showdown
  • Show All Community Cards - See what would have come if you stayed in
  • Peek at Opponent Cards - Practice reading situations with full information
  • Computer Cards Face Up - Play with perfect information
  • Pause After Deal - Stop after deal, flop, turn, or river to analyze situations
  • Lock Dealer Button - Practice specific positions (button, blinds, UTG)
  • Never Go Broke - Get chips back to continue practicing
  • Modify Players - Change names, stacks, cards, or AI types mid-game

Game Preferences

  • Card Size - Standard or large cards
  • Auto Deal - Automatically deal the next hand
  • All-In Pause - Show win probability when all-in
  • Face Down Hole Cards - Practice memorizing your hand
  • Skip After Fold - Jump to end of hand after folding
  • Hands Per Hour - Control tournament clock speed
  • Action Delay - Adjust AI decision speed
  • Deck Customization - Choose or upload custom deck designs

In-Game Help

Comprehensive help system covering game rules, strategy concepts, poker etiquette, and tournament hosting tips. Access anytime via the Help button.
