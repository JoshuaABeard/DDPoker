Screenshots

Browse screenshots showing DD Poker's interface and features. For the best look, download the game and try it yourself.

Main Menu - Quick access to Practice, Online, Analysis, Poker Clock, Calculator, and Help.

Player Profiles - Automatic tracking of tournament results, wins, and prize money. Support for multiple player profiles.

Tournament Formats - 15 pre-defined tournaments including various blind structures and formats.

Tournament Editor - Full customization of levels, blinds, antes, buy-ins, rebuys, add-ons, and payouts.

Game Interface - DD Dashboard shows rank, tables remaining, blinds/antes, level timer, pot odds, and hand strength.

AI Advisor - Context-aware advice with detailed explanations of recommendations.

Hand Analysis - Complete hand history tracking and statistical analysis of your play.

Poker Clock - Tournament timer with automated blind tracking and level management for home games.

Online Lobby - Chat and coordinate with other players before, during, and after tournaments.

Showdown Calculator - Calculate exact all-in odds with Monte Carlo simulation.

Flop/Turn/River Analysis - Project hand improvement probabilities.

Hand Ladder - Compare your hand against all possible opponent holdings.

Hand Group Simulations - Test your hand against customizable opponent ranges.
